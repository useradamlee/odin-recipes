Chicken Rice

Description

Chicken rice is a dish that is commonly found in Southeast Asia, particularly in Malaysia, Singapore and Thailand. It is a simple dish that consists of poached chicken and seasoned rice, served with some condiments. The chicken is usually poached in a broth of ginger, garlic and pandan leaves, which gives it a fragrant aroma. The rice is cooked in the same broth, which gives it a rich and savoury flavour. The dish is usually served with some chilli sauce, sliced cucumber and a bowl of chicken broth. It is a comforting and satisfying meal that is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 4 boneless, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 1 tbsp rendered chicken fat or neutral oil
  • 1 tbsp minced ginger
  • 1 clove garlic minced
  • 1/2 small shallot finely diced
  • 1 cup white jasmine rice
  • 1 cup chicken stock low sodium
  • 2 green onions whole
